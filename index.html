Advice for those moving into Independent School/Headship

A Stoic Approach; leading with wisdom, morality, courage, and moderation or“Just keep in mind: the more we value things outside our control, the less control we have”. Epictetus

A little about the search process

4 or more attempts (NAIS survey) Nobody asks the author how many rejections they received before their first publication. They just want to know what it's like to be successful Most candidates are “stretching” their skills (hard to feel qualified) The most common reason for a candidate being rejected after the final round was a lack of previous experience as a head (NAIS Survey) All schools want a 35 year old leader with 20 years of experience so be yourself (best version) Consultants work with (for) schools and educational organizations to devise the best type of search (Carney Sandoe Website)
